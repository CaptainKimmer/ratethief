My story

I built RateThief.com to make refinancing feel simple, honest, and fast. I’ve seen how confusing mortgage shopping can be — and how easy it is for people to get stuck with the wrong loan, the wrong fees, or the wrong timing.

My life has been anything but typical: I went through cancer, found my footing again through the mortgage industry, then faced cancer again. In between, I sailed solo across the Atlantic during hurricane season. That experience taught me a lot about pressure, clarity, and doing things the right way. I brought that same mindset into mortgages.

Why I created RateThief.com

  • To help homeowners quickly understand their options without the usual runaround.
  • To connect people with the right lender for their goals (lower payment, cash out, payoff debt, HELOC, purchase, etc.).
  • To keep the process straightforward, transparent, and focused on what actually helps you.
Note: RateThief.com is a lead-generation and information service. We are not a lender and do not make credit decisions. If you submit your information, you may be contacted by phone, email, and/or text about mortgage products and services.
