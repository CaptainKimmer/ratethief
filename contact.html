What RateThief.com is

RateThief.com is built to connect homeowners directly with me and a small group of trusted loan officers — so you can explore options without getting bombarded by spam calls, random texts, and endless “rate quote” follow-ups.

The pirate “Rate Thief” theme comes from my love of sailing — and the belief that finding you a great mortgage rate should feel like we’re stealing it (the legal kind) from the market.

The goal is simple: build a long-term relationship with you as a homeowner and help you make smart mortgage moves over time — refinances, cash-out, debt consolidation, HELOCs, or purchases — when it actually saves you money across your entire home-owning life.

Sailing across the Atlantic solo — one of the many hard things that shaped how I approach life and business.

My story

I had a great life, then I got cancer. I was saved — literally — by the mortgage industry and the people in it. After recovering, I got cancer again.

Somewhere in the middle of all of that, I sailed across the Atlantic Ocean solo during hurricane season. It changed how I see risk, preparation, discipline, and long-term thinking.

I came back with one mission: become the best mortgage professional I can be, build something real, start a family, and help homeowners win long-term — not just on one transaction.
