Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

RateThief.com (“RateThief,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and the choices you may have.

1) What this policy covers

This policy applies to information collected through the RateThief.com website and any forms or pages we link to that collect information for mortgage-related inquiries (collectively, the “Site”).

2) Information we collect

A. Information you provide

  • Contact information (such as name, email address, and phone number).
  • Property and loan information (such as occupancy, property type, estimated value, current mortgage details, and refinance goals).
  • Any other details you choose to submit in a form or message field.

B. Information collected automatically

  • Device and usage data (such as browser type, device type, operating system, pages viewed, and general interaction with the Site).
  • Network data (such as IP address and approximate location derived from IP (typically city/region)).
  • Cookie and similar technology data as described below.

3) How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Connect you with one or more participating mortgage lenders or service partners that may be able to assist with your request.
  • Respond to your inquiry and communicate with you about mortgage-related products and services.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Site, including troubleshooting and performance monitoring.
  • Measure marketing performance and understand which advertising or pages are most helpful to visitors.
  • Help protect the Site, prevent fraud, and enforce our policies.
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

4) How we share information

RateThief.com is a lead-intake and matching website and is not a lender. We may share information as follows:

  • With participating lenders and partners: If you submit information through the Site, we may provide that information to one or more independent mortgage lenders and related partners so they can evaluate your request and contact you.
  • With service providers: We may use vendors to support the Site (such as hosting, analytics, security, and marketing tools). These vendors may process information on our behalf to provide services to us.
  • For legal and safety reasons: We may disclose information if required by law, to respond to legal process, to protect rights and safety, or to investigate suspected fraud or misuse.
  • Business transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Not “selling” for unrelated marketing: We do not share your personal information with unrelated third parties for their own independent marketing of non-mortgage products. However, like most websites, we may use advertising and analytics tools that involve cookies or similar technologies (see the next section).

5) Cookies, analytics, and advertising technologies

We may use cookies, pixels, tags, and similar technologies to help the Site function, understand traffic, and measure advertising effectiveness. Some of these technologies may be provided by third parties (for example, analytics or advertising platforms).

Your choices:

  • You can control cookies through your browser settings (including blocking or deleting cookies).
  • If you limit cookies, some parts of the Site may not work as intended.
  • You may also be able to adjust advertising preferences through the settings on the platforms where you see our ads (for example, Google or Meta).

6) Communications and consent

If you submit your information through the Site, you may be contacted by RateThief.com and/or participating lenders or partners by phone, email, and/or text message about mortgage products and services. Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier.

7) Data retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary to support the purposes described in this policy, including to operate the Site, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

8) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. No website or internet transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9) Third-party links

The Site may link to third-party websites or services (including lender sites or external forms). We do not control those third parties, and their privacy practices are governed by their own policies. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing information.

10) Children’s privacy

The Site is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11) Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information, and in some cases to opt out of certain types of data processing. Because our processes may evolve, the specific steps for making a request may be described on the Site from time to time.

12) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. If we make changes, we will post the updated version on this page and revise the effective date above.
