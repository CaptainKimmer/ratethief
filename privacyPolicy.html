Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

RateThief.com (“RateThief,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we share it, and the choices you may have when using our website and related pages or forms we link to (collectively, the “Site”).

1) Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through the Site. It does not cover the privacy practices of third parties, including participating lenders, service partners, or platforms we do not control.

2) Information we collect

A. Information you provide

  • Contact details such as your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Property and loan details such as occupancy, property type, estimated value, current mortgage information, and refinance goals.
  • Any other information you choose to submit through forms or fields on the Site.

B. Information collected automatically

  • Device and usage data such as browser type, device type, operating system, pages viewed, time spent, and general interaction signals.
  • Network data such as IP address and approximate location derived from IP (typically city/region).
  • Cookie and similar technology data as described in Section 5 below.

C. Information from other sources

In limited cases, we may receive information from service providers that help us measure traffic, prevent fraud, or evaluate advertising performance. This information is typically aggregated or technical in nature.

3) How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Site.
  • Respond to your request and communicate with you about mortgage-related products and services.
  • Connect you with one or more independent participating mortgage lenders or service partners that may be able to assist with your inquiry.
  • Measure marketing performance and understand how visitors use the Site.
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, abuse, or security incidents.
  • Comply with applicable laws and respond to lawful requests.

4) How we share information

RateThief.com is a lead-intake and matching website and is not a lender. We do not originate, fund, or make mortgage loans, and we do not make credit decisions.

  • With participating lenders and partners: If you submit information through the Site, we may share it with one or more independent mortgage lenders or service partners so they can evaluate your request and contact you.
  • With service providers: We may share information with vendors that support the Site (such as hosting, analytics, security, marketing measurement, and technical operations). These vendors process information on our behalf.
  • For legal and safety purposes: We may disclose information when we believe it is necessary to comply with law, protect rights and safety, enforce policies, or investigate suspected fraud or misuse.
  • Business transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, reorganization, financing, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

No unrelated marketing list sharing: We do not share your personal information with unrelated third parties for their own independent marketing of non-mortgage products. However, like most websites, we may use analytics and advertising technologies that involve cookies and similar tools (see Section 5).

5) Cookies and similar technologies

We may use cookies, pixels, tags, and similar technologies (“Cookies”) to help the Site function, understand traffic, and measure advertising effectiveness. Some Cookies are set by us, and others may be set by third-party service providers.

How we use Cookies

  • Remember preferences and basic site functionality.
  • Understand site usage and improve performance.
  • Measure advertising and marketing effectiveness.
  • Help detect fraud and protect the Site.

Your choices

  • You can manage Cookies through your browser settings (including blocking or deleting Cookies).
  • If you block Cookies, some features may not work as intended.
  • You may also be able to control ad personalization through settings provided by advertising platforms where you see ads.

6) Communications and consent

If you submit your information through the Site, you may be contacted by RateThief.com and/or participating lenders or partners by phone, email, and/or text message regarding mortgage-related products and services. Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier. You can opt out of text messages by replying “STOP” to a text message you receive (where applicable).

7) Data retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to support the purposes described in this policy, including operating the Site, meeting legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing agreements.

8) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission over the internet or method of storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9) Third-party links and external forms

The Site may link to third-party websites or services (including lender sites or external forms). We do not control those third parties, and their privacy practices are governed by their own policies. Please review their policies before providing information.

10) Children’s privacy

The Site is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11) Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of certain personal information, and in some cases to opt out of certain types of data processing.

How to submit a request: To submit a privacy-related request, please use the contact method provided on this website. We may need to verify your request before completing it, and certain requests may be limited or subject to exceptions under applicable law.

12) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will post the updated version on this page and update the effective date above.
